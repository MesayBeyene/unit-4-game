Cristal Collecton Game

    Instructions

  • You will be given a random number at the bigining of the game
  • There are four Crystals below. By clicking on the crystals you will add a specific
    amount of poinsts to your total score
  • You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if
    your total score gets above the random number
  • The value of each crystal in hidden form you until you click it.
  • Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
